Smart Elevators

  • OOP,Java

The project focuses on the challenge of optimizing elevator scheduling. Given a call for the elevator from the source floor to the destination floor - the system will want to schedule the elevator that will minimize the arrival time. More generally, it is said that given a collection of calls to elevators in time, we would like to define a strategy for assigning elevators to calls that will minimize the total arrival time for all the calls.
